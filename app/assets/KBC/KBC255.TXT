Psalm 134
order:1,2,1,2
=
Behold, bless the LORD,
All servants of the LORD,
Who serve by night
In the house of the LORD! 
Lift up your hands to the sanctuary
And bless the LORD. 
And bless the LORD. 
=
May the LORD
Bless you from Zion,
He who made heaven and earth.
May the LORD
Bless you from Zion,
He who made heaven and earth.
